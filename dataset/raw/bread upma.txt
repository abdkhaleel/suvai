Name: Bread Upma Recipe
Tamil Name: பிரெட் உப்புமா (Bread Uppuma)
Category: breakfast
Sub Category: tiffin
Diet: vegetarian
Difficulty: 
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 3

Description:
Bread upma recipe is a quick and easy variation made with bread cubes, onions, tomatoes, spices and herbs. It comes together in about 30 minutes. It is great for breakfast, brunch or an evening snack. Use any soft bread – whole wheat, multi grain, brown or white bread. Fresh or a few days old bread both work well.

Ingredients:
- 3 cups chopped bread cubes or 5 to 6 bread slices – chopped in cubes
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
- ½ teaspoon cumin seeds
- ⅓ cup finely chopped onions or 1 medium-sized
- ½ teaspoon finely chopped green chilies or 1 green chili
- 1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger or ¾ to 1 inch ginger
- 6 to 7 curry leaves – chopped
- ½ cup finely chopped tomatoes or 2 medium-sized
- ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
- ¼ teaspoon red chili powder
- 1 pinch asafoetida (hing) – optional
- 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves (cilantro)
- salt as required

Steps:
1. Chop the bread slices in cubes. Finely chop the onions, green chilli, ginger, tomatoes, curry leaves and coriander leaves. Keep aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle and pop.
3. Then add cumin seeds. Stir and saute till the cumin seeds also crackle.
4. Add chopped onions. Saute till they turn translucent.
5. Add finely chopped green chilies, finely chopped ginger and chopped curry leaves. Stir and saute for 10 to 12 seconds.
6. Then add finely chopped tomatoes. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder and a pinch of asafoetida (optional). Add salt as required.
7. Stir and saute this onion-tomato-spices mixture till the tomatoes soften and you see oil releasing from the sides. The mixture should be slightly dry.
8. Add the bread cubes. Mix the bread well with the rest of the mixture.
9. On a low to medium flame, saute the bread cubes for 4 to 5 minutes till they become slightly crisp from the edges.
10. Then add chopped coriander leaves. Mix and serve bread upma hot. While serving you can drizzle some lemon juice on top.

Tips:
- Use any variety of soft bread. Pav (dinner rolls) also works well.
- Better health option would be whole wheat bread, multi grain or ragi bread.
- Reduce or increase spices according to taste. For kids, skip green chillies and red chilli powder.
- Add some roasted cashews or roasted peanuts for crunch.

Variations:
- Make with pav (dinner rolls).
- Add other veggies like capsicum, carrots etc.

Commonly served with:
as is (drizzle some lemon juice while eating). Can be served with tea or coffee as evening snack.
