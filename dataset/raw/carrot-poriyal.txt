Name: Carrot Poriyal
Tamil Name: காரட் பொரியல்
Category: kootu-poriyal
Sub Category: poriyal
Diet: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free
Difficulty: easy
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 4
Description:
Carrot Poriyal is a simple, everyday Tamil Nadu style stir-fried carrot dish. Fresh carrots are cooked with basic spices and finished with grated coconut. This quick and healthy poriyal is a staple side dish in Tamil meals and pairs perfectly with rice, sambar, and rasam. It is a no onion no garlic recipe.

Ingredients:

* Carrot (காரட் - காரட்): 2 cups (finely chopped)
* Sesame oil / Gingelly oil (நல்லெண்ணெய் - நல்லெண்ணெய்): 2 tbsp
* Mustard seeds (கடுகு - கடுகு): 1 tsp
* Urad dal (உளுந்து பருப்பு - உளுந்து பருப்பு): 1 tsp
* Green chilli (பச்சை மிளகாய் - பச்சை மிளகாய்): 1 (chopped)
* Curry leaves (கறிவேப்பிலை - கறிவேப்பிலை): 8 to 9
* Turmeric powder (மஞ்சள் தூள் - மஞ்சள் தூள்): ¼ tsp
* Asafoetida (பெருங்காயம் - பெருங்காயம்): 1 pinch
* Grated coconut (தேங்காய் துருவல் - தேங்காய் துருவல்): ⅓ cup
* Salt (உப்பு - உப்பு): to taste
* Water (தண்ணீர் - தண்ணீர்): ½ cup (or as needed)

Steps:

1. Heat gingelly oil in a kadai or pan on low heat. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
2. Add urad dal and fry until golden, stirring continuously to prevent burning.
3. Add chopped green chilli and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
4. Add finely chopped carrots, turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well.
5. Add ½ cup water and stir. Cover and cook on low heat until carrots are tender.
6. Check every 4-5 minutes and add more water if needed.
7. Once carrots are cooked and water evaporates, add grated coconut.
8. Mix thoroughly and cook for 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat.
9. Serve hot or warm. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves if desired.

Tips:

* Finely chop the carrots for faster and even cooking.
* Use fresh tender carrots for best taste and colour.
* Do not add too much water to keep the poriyal dry.
* You can use coconut oil for a more authentic flavour.

Variations:
Carrot Beans Poriyal, Carrot Cabbage Poriyal, Grated Carrot Poriyal

Commonly served with:
Steamed rice, Sambar, Rasam, Curd rice
