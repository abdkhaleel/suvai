Name: Sukku Malli Coffee
Tamil Name: சுக்கு மல்லி காபி
Category: drinks
Sub Category: coffee
Diet: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free
Difficulty: easy
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 8 servings (powder batch)
Description:
Sukku Malli Coffee, also known as Chukku Kaapi or Dry Ginger Coffee, is a traditional South Indian herbal beverage made from dried ginger, coriander seeds, black pepper and cardamom. It is a soothing, aromatic drink with numerous health benefits — it aids digestion, boosts immunity, relieves cold and cough, and improves blood circulation. A popular alternative to regular tea or coffee, it is especially comforting during rainy seasons and winter months.

Ingredients:

For Coffee Powder:
* Dried ginger / Sukku (சுக்கு - சுக்கு): 1 cup (roughly crushed)
* Coriander seeds / Malli (மல்லி விதை - மல்லி விதை): ½ cup
* Black peppercorns (மிளகு - மிளகு): 1 tbsp
* Cardamom pods (ஏலக்காய் - ஏலக்காய்): 6
* Cinnamon stick (பட்டை - பட்டை): 1 small stick
* Nutmeg powder (ஜாதிக்காய் பொடி - ஜாதிக்காய் பொடி): 1 tsp
* Dried tulsi leaves (காய்ந்த துளசி இலை - காய்ந்த துளசி இலை): handful (optional)

For Serving (per cup):
* Water (தண்ணீர் - தண்ணீர்): 1 cup
* Sukku Malli powder (சுக்கு மல்லி பொடி - சுக்கு மல்லி பொடி): 1 tsp
* Palm jaggery / Palm sugar candy (பனை வெல்லம் / பனங்கற்கண்டு - பனை வெல்லம் / பனங்கற்கண்டு): 1 tbsp
* Milk (பால் - பால்): ½ cup (optional, for milky version)

Steps:

1. Rinse dried ginger well and dry it. Peel the skin and discard.
2. Using a mortar and pestle, roughly crush the dried ginger into smaller pieces.
3. To a dry pan, add coriander seeds, black pepper and cardamom pods.
4. Dry roast on low-medium flame until golden and aromatic. Do not burn.
5. Add the crushed dry ginger and give a quick sauté. Switch off.
6. Add dried tulsi leaves and nutmeg powder towards the end. Roast for another 30 seconds.
7. Cool completely, then transfer to a mixer jar.
8. Grind to a fine powder. Cool down completely.
9. Store in an airtight container in a dry place. Use within one month for best flavour.

To Prepare Coffee (Without Milk):
10. Boil 1 cup water in a saucepan.
11. Add 1 tsp Sukku Malli powder and 1 tbsp palm jaggery.
12. Let it simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
13. Strain the mixture and discard the remains.
14. Serve hot.

To Prepare Coffee (With Milk):
15. Boil ½ cup water and ½ cup milk together.
16. Add 1 tsp Sukku Malli powder.
17. Let it come to a rolling boil, simmer for 1 minute.
18. Add 1 tbsp palm jaggery. Switch off.
19. Strain and serve hot.

Tips:

* Crush dried ginger roughly before grinding — adding whole pieces can damage your mixer.
* Dry roast spices on low-medium flame to preserve aroma and prevent burning.
* Cool ingredients completely before grinding to prevent moisture in the powder.
* Palm sugar candy (panangarkandu) is the best sweetener for cold and cough relief.
* You can use karupatti (palm jaggery), regular jaggery or even honey (add only to lukewarm coffee).
* Add a cardamom pod while boiling for extra aroma.
* Sukku Malli Coffee is best consumed warm for maximum health benefits.
* The powder can be stored for up to a month in an airtight container.

Variations:
Sukku Coffee with Milk, Chukku Kaapi (Kerala style), Pepper Sukku Coffee, Tulsi Sukku Coffee

Commonly served with:
Morning Beverage, Evening Drink, Yoga Class Refreshment, Rainy Day Comfort, Cold & Cough Remedy
