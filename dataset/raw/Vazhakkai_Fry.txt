Name: Vazhakkai Fry
Tamil Name: வாழைக்காய் வறுவல்
Category: kootu-poriyal
Sub Category: poriyal
Diet: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free
Difficulty: easy
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Servings: 3
Description:
Vazhakkai Fry aka poriyal is a Tamil Nadu style side dish of stir fried unripe bananas with spices and herbs. Sliced unripe green banana is tossed with mustard seeds, cumin seeds and sambar powder, for a flavorful and delicious dish. It is eaten as a side with rice, rasam, sambar or kootu. For a simpler meal you may serve it with lemon rice, curd rice or curry leaves rice.

Ingredients:

* Raw banana / Vazhakkai (வாழைக்காய் - வாழைக்காய்): 2 medium (approx. 400 grams)
* Oil (எண்ணெய் - எண்ணெய்): 2 tbsp
* Mustard seeds (கடுகு - கடுகு): 1 tsp
* Cumin seeds (சீரகம் - சீரகம்): ½ tsp
* Urad dal (உளுந்து பருப்பு - உளுந்து பருப்பு): ½ tsp
* Dried red chilli (வற்றல் மிளகாய் - வற்றல் மிளகாய்): 1
* Curry leaves (கறிவேப்பிலை - கறிவேப்பிலை): 1 sprig
* Asafoetida / Hing (பெருங்காயம் - பெருங்காயம்): 1 pinch
* Turmeric powder (மஞ்சள் தூள் - மஞ்சள் தூள்): ¼ tsp
* Sambar powder (சாம்பார் பொடி - சாம்பார் பொடி): 1½ tsp
* Red chilli powder (மிளகாய் தூள் - மிளகாய் தூள்): ¼ tsp (optional, to taste)
* Salt (உப்பு - உப்பு): ½ tsp (or to taste)
* Water (தண்ணீர் - தண்ணீர்): 1 to 2 tbsp (as needed)

Steps:

1. Wash the raw bananas and chop off both the ends. Peel the skin completely.
2. Slice the bananas evenly (not too thick or too thin) and add them to a pot of water to prevent discoloration.
3. Heat a wide pan with oil. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilli and urad dal.
4. When the dal turns golden, throw in the curry leaves and sprinkle asafoetida.
5. Lower the flame and add turmeric powder, sambar powder and red chilli powder. Mix it well.
6. Drain the banana slices and add them to the pan. Mix well and fry for 2 minutes.
7. Sprinkle 1 to 2 tbsps of water. Mix well.
8. Cover and cook on a very low to medium flame until the banana slices turn tender/soft. If needed sprinkle more water and then stir and cook covered again.
9. Check if the vazhakkai has cooked properly.
10. Sprinkle salt evenly. Sprinkle another tbsp of water if needed (helps salt dissolve and coat well). Cover and cook for 2 to 3 mins.
11. Remove from heat and serve hot with rice or variety rice.

Tips:

* Choose tender and young vazhakkai for best results. Mature green bananas may require more water.
* Always use firm, fresh looking and fat green bananas. Avoid bananas that have begun to ripen.
* Using a wider pan helps to get these stir fried easily without sticking.
* You may chop them to bite sized pieces instead of slicing.
* Store bought sambar podi can be extremely hot, so adjust quantity to keep spice levels moderate.
* Soak sliced bananas in water immediately to prevent discoloration/oxidization.

Variations:
Aratikaya Fry, Masala Banana Curry, Green Banana Kura, Vazhakkai Poriyal with Coconut

Commonly served with:
Steamed rice, Rasam, Sambar, Kootu, Lemon rice, Curd rice, Curry leaves rice
