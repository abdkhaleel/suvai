Name: Sorakkai Kootu
Tamil Name: சுரைக்காய் கூட்டு
Category: kootu-poriyal
Sub Category: kootu
Diet: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free
Difficulty: easy
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Servings: 4
Description:
Sorakkai Kootu is a mild, comforting and healthy Tamil Nadu style bottle gourd kootu made with moong dal and coconut. This simple everyday dish is light on the stomach and pairs beautifully with steamed rice, sambar or rasam. It is a regular lunch recipe in most Tamil households.

Ingredients:

* Sorakkai / Bottle Gourd (சுரைக்காய் - சுரைக்காய்): 1 medium (500 grams)
* Moong dal (பாசிப்பருப்பு - பாசிப்பருப்பு): ⅓ cup
* Turmeric powder (மஞ்சள் தூள் - மஞ்சள் தூள்): ¼ tsp
* Salt (உப்பு - உப்பு): to taste

For Coconut Paste:
* Grated coconut (தேங்காய் துருவல் - தேங்காய் துருவல்): ½ cup
* Green chilli (பச்சை மிளகாய் - பச்சை மிளகாய்): 1
* Cumin seeds (சீரகம் - சீரகம்): 1 tsp

For Tempering:
* Coconut oil or gingelly oil (தேங்காய் எண்ணெய் அல்லது நல்லெண்ணெய் - தேங்காய் எண்ணெய் அல்லது நல்லெண்ணெய்): 2 tsp
* Mustard seeds (கடுகு - கடுகு): ½ tsp
* Urad dal (உளுந்து பருப்பு - உளுந்து பருப்பு): 1 tsp
* Curry leaves (கறிவேப்பிலை - கறிவேப்பிலை): 1 sprig
* Asafoetida (பெருங்காயம் - பெருங்காயம்): 1 pinch

Steps:

1. Rinse moong dal and pressure cook with ¾ cup water and a pinch of turmeric for 2-3 whistles until soft. Mash lightly and set aside.
2. Peel the bottle gourd, remove the seeds and chop into small cubes.
3. In a pan, add chopped sorakkai, turmeric powder, and little water. Cook covered until the vegetable becomes soft.
4. Grind grated coconut, green chilli and cumin seeds into a smooth paste with little water.
5. Add the cooked moong dal and ground coconut paste to the cooked sorakkai. Mix gently.
6. Add salt and simmer for 3-4 minutes until the kootu comes together. Adjust consistency with little hot water if needed.
7. Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
8. Add urad dal and fry until golden. Add curry leaves and asafoetida. Pour the tempering over the kootu.
9. Mix well and serve hot.

Tips:

* Choose tender bottle gourd for better taste.
* Do not overcook the vegetable after adding dal to retain texture.
* You can replace moong dal with toor dal for a different flavour.
* This kootu thickens as it cools, so keep it slightly loose.

Variations:
Sorakkai Kootu with chana dal, Mixed vegetable sorakkai kootu, Sorakkai paruppu usili kootu

Commonly served with:
Steamed rice, Sambar, Rasam, Curd rice
