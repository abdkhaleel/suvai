Name: Beans Poriyal
Tamil Name: பீன்ஸ் பொரியல்
Category: kootu-poriyal
Sub Category: poriyal
Diet: vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free
Difficulty: easy
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes
Servings: 3
Description:
Beans Poriyal is a simple, everyday Tamil Nadu style stir-fried beans dish. Fresh green beans are cooked with coconut, mustard seeds, and basic spices. This quick and healthy poriyal is a staple side dish in Tamil meals and pairs perfectly with rice, sambar, and rasam.

Ingredients:

* French Beans (பீன்ஸ் - பீன்ஸ்): 250 grams
* Onion (வெங்காயம் - வெங்காயம்): 1 small (optional)
* Grated coconut (தேங்காய் துருவல் - தேங்காய் துருவல்): 3 to 4 tbsp
* Salt (உப்பு - உப்பு): to taste

For Tempering:
* Oil (எண்ணெய் - எண்ணெய்): 2 tsp
* Mustard seeds (கடுகு - கடுகு): ½ tsp
* Urad dal (உளுந்து பருப்பு - உளுந்து பருப்பு): 1 tsp
* Dried red chilli (காய்ந்த மிளகாய் - காய்ந்த மிளகாய்): 1 (broken)
* Curry leaves (கறிவேப்பிலை - கறிவேப்பிலை): 1 sprig
* Asafoetida (பெருங்காயம் - பெருங்காயம்): 1 pinch

Steps:

1. Wash and trim both ends of the beans. Chop them into small pieces.
2. Heat oil in a pan or kadai. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
3. Add urad dal and fry until golden brown.
4. Add broken red chilli, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Sauté for a few seconds.
5. Add chopped beans and sauté for 1 minute.
6. Sprinkle little water, cover and cook on medium-low flame until beans are soft (stir occasionally).
7. Add salt and mix well. Cook for another 2 minutes.
8. Add grated coconut and mix thoroughly. Cook for 1-2 minutes until everything is well combined.
9. Remove from heat and serve hot.

Tips:

* Chop beans evenly for uniform cooking.
* Do not add too much water — beans should retain a slight crunch.
* Fresh grated coconut gives the best taste.
* You can add a pinch of sugar or jaggery for mild sweetness (optional).

Variations:
Beans Carrot Poriyal, Beans with onion, Beans Paruppu Usili

Commonly served with:
Steamed rice, Sambar, Rasam, Curd rice
